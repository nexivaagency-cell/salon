Good to Know

Frequently asked.

Do I need to book in advance?+

Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend booking at least a day ahead for colour, bridal or spa services so we can hold the right specialist's time.

Is a bridal trial included in the package?+

Yes. Every bridal package includes one full trial session for hair and makeup before the wedding day itself.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?+

Memberships are billed monthly with no lock-in. You can pause or cancel from your account at any time.

What products do you use?+

We use professional, salon-exclusive product lines for colour, treatments and styling — the same ones available in our product shelf.
